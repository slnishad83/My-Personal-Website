How to Revoke Permissions

×

For your security, web browsers and operating systems do not allow apps to secretly revoke permissions once granted. You must do it manually in your settings:

Android: Long-press the app icon > App info > Permissions. You can allow or deny Camera, Microphone, and Notifications there.
Browser (Desktop): Click the lock/tune icon near the address bar. Change Camera, Microphone, and Notification permissions to Block.

After changing settings, reopen the app and check your permissions again.
