Call Network Settings

TURN helps voice and video calls connect across mobile data, office Wi-Fi, strict routers, and different networks.

Simple free setup
  1. Open Metered OpenRelay and create a free account.
  2. Copy your app URL, for example yourappname.metered.live.
  3. Copy your TURN API key.
  4. Paste both below, then click Test & Save Metered Settings.

No card should be needed for the free allowance. If your app grows heavily, TURN usage may need a paid plan later.

Advanced: paste TURN server JSON here only if your provider gives direct TURN username/password details.
